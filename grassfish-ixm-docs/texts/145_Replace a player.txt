Titel: Replace a player
URL: https://docs.grassfish.com/docs/replace-a-player
Zeitpunkt: 2025-07-17 18:40:07
==================================================

To replace a player, perform the following steps: Start the player. Note the TPID that you see on the screen. Open the provisioning website via the custom URL. Optionally, select a language from the menu at the bottom left. On the provisioning website, select Replace player . Enter the following data: Application : enter your IXM One application name. User : enter your IXM One username. Password : enter your IXM One password. Click Log in . Enter the TPID and Box-ID of the player. Select Continue . Review the player details and click Confirm . Select the time zone from the dropdown menu and click Confirm . Select the screen rotation and click Confirm . Select Finish to finish the initialization.
