Titel: Use player options and settings
URL: https://docs.grassfish.com/docs/menu-window
Zeitpunkt: 2025-07-17 18:43:32
==================================================

The menu window provides general information and access to additional options and system settings. Click on the following icons for additional options: Start the player. Open the settings . Select the screen orientation: Landscape Landscape reverse Portrait Portrait reverse Auto Reset the player. Exit player. This shuts down the player software.
