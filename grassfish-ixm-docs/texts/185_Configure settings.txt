Titel: Configure settings
URL: https://docs.grassfish.com/docs/post-installation-settings
Zeitpunkt: 2025-07-17 18:41:55
==================================================

After installing the software, you can configure the player settings. To do so, perform the following steps: Open the app drawer. Locate the GF Device Owner app and open it. In the subsequent dialog, click Open settings . Click GF Device Owner and turn on Allow access to manage all files . Turn on the same setting for GF Player and Host (if installed). Ensure that All files access is set to Allowed for these apps. Click the back arrow to return to the Device Owner app. The system performs an automatic reboot. Note If the dialog for file access permissions is not available, the file access permission is already granted. In that case, turn on the setting Player in kiosk mode and click REBOOT . Grant permissions After the reboot, you can grant permissions that may not have been granted yet, based on the firmware of your device. To grant permissions, perform the following steps: Click Open next to the permissions that have not been granted. Click Open next to the required settings and Allow . After each setting, click the back arrow or right-click to return to the Permissions page. After all permissions have been granted, the player starts the InitBox. The installation is now complete.
